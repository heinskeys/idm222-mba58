Fall Term 2022

IDM211: User Experience Design

Overview

In IDM 211 - User Experience Design I, students were assigned a model website (for an Architecture Firm) to redesign based on specific prompts. Each prompt allowed one week for the redesign within the chosen theme. It was mandated that the websiteâ€™s content, icons, and scope remained unchanged.

Prompt I: Graffiti
View Figma Prototype

This prompt required a redesign of the website in a Graffiti-inspired style. I opted for collage-esque graphics paired with a bold brush font. To this day, the resulting design stands as one of my favorite hero designs.

Prompt II: Constructivism
View Figma Prototype

In response to this prompt, students were tasked with redesigning the website in the Constructivist style, prominent in Soviet art and design. I chose to embrace this concept by framing the website akin to a Communist poster, complete with direct references to Soviet leaders.
