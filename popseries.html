Pop! Poster Series

2021 - Present

Graphic Design

Overview

Pop! Poster Series is an ongoing personal project aimed at creating posters for various pop culture media, characters, and artists that capture my interest. The ultimate objective of this endeavor is to adorn an entire wall of my room with the posters I've meticulously crafted.

Doja Cat 2021-22

This particular poster had been brewing in my mind for some time, sparked by a visit to the Philadelphia Museum of Art. There, I found myself captivated by Renaissance-style scenery. Armed with environmental brushes, I embarked on creating this piece, incorporating elements from Michelangelo’s "The Creation of Adam" and Raphael’s "The Triumph of Galatea".

Genshin Character Profiles, 2022

Inspiration for this series struck from the allure of the Golden Ratio. Each poster predominantly features a composition of rectangles meticulously aligned with those found in a Golden Ratio diagram. I find this approach appealing as it lends a sense of harmony and design uniformity across the series, resulting in a more cohesive collection.
